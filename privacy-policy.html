Overview

Our fundamental philosophy is simple: We do not collect your personal data. This App is designed to perform backtesting locally on your device without compromising your identity or financial information.

The App does not collect, store, share, or process any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). Specifically, we do not access:

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no risk of your personal information being leaked from our servers. All your strategy backtesting data remains on your local device.
